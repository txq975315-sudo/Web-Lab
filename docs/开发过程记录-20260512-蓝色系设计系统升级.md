  background-color: #e6f0f8;
  background-image: url('/backgrounds/bg-figma-sky.jpg');

  background-position: center;
  background-repeat: no-repeat;

## 🔍 关键决策

1. **背景图选择**：选择了第二张图（浅蓝色天空+米色云朵），与Figma截图最贴近
